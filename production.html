Production

Production department at Unexo Labs is manufacturing tablets and capsules of different therapeutic categories. The production department consists of following sections :

  • Penicillin section.

  • Cephalosporin section.

  • Quinolone section

  • Narcotics/Psychotropics section.

  • General tablets section.

  • Anti-tuberculosis section.

  • Steroid section.

  • General capsule section.

  • Raw material section.

The following manufacturing facilities are being used.

  • Fluidized bed dryer.

  • Mixers.

  • Fully automated compression machines.

  • Fully automated capsule filling machines.

  • Blister machines.

The critical manufacturing process is done under controlled environmental condition.
